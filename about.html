TEAM WORK

WORK TOGETHER

Teamwork is the basis to success. Learning while having fun together will help your child succeed in group based projects.
The more hands working together, for a common goal, the better! This is Native strategy to hands on projects. It's a strategy recommended to motivate kids who can't sustain efforts, especially childrem seem overwhelmed by everyday tasks

OUR CURRICULUM

Our curriculum is based on a program model that focuses on learning enhancement, healthy discussions and hands-on play. Building competence and confidence in children.
Worshops run every regular school day, and after school programs are from 3 p.m. to 6 p.m.
All programs are supervised and structured in a safe environment.

GAMES

LEARN THROUGH GAMES

One of our most fun programs is the Native American games program. This program was developed by native people who are expert in games which focus on having fun and developing team skills and hand and eye cordination.
Students identify game they like to play most and have fun playing and developing skills.
After game playing the group discuss ways these skills would help Native American people of long ago and how the skill could be useful today.
