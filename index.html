WORKSHOPS FOR KIDS

These are hands-on workshops whose objectives are to help you understand better the culture of the first American. All of our workshops are hand-on, project-based, and problem-solving is at the heart the workshop.

After School Programs are focused on various themes, as for example story telling, innovation and are hands on.

With the rich American Indian history which we are focusing in it is never the same and we strive to always be engaging and fun.
All classes are tailored to the level of the students from preschool to sixth grade. And we have weekend family workshops which allow all the family to enjoyable working togethe and bonding.
